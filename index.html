The race for the last spots

Every team that has not yet clinched a Round-of-32 place and is not yet eliminated — the live bubble. They are chasing the open berths: the still-undecided runner-up spots in the live groups, plus the final best-third places (8 of the 12 third-placed teams advance). A team can grab a spot either by climbing to 2nd in its group or by finishing as one of the eight best thirds — which is why teams currently sitting 3rd and 4th appear here. The chance bar is the share of all remaining result combinations (equally weighted) in which the team reaches the Round of 32 — a combinatorial guide, not a betting line. A ★ win & in tag means the team controls its own destiny: win the last game and it qualifies no matter what else happens. Tiebreakers follow the new 2026 rules: points, then head-to-head among level teams (points → GD → goals), and only then overall GD and goals — a reversal from past World Cups where overall GD came first.
